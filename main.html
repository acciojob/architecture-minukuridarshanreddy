CAPTAINS OF STRAW HAT PIRATES AND ROGER PIRATES

The Straw Hat Pirates, in order of joining the crew, are: Monkey D. Luffy, Roronoa Zoro, Nami, Usopp, Vinsmoke Sanji, Tony Tony Chopper, Nico Robin, Franky, Brook, and Jinbe. 		"One Piece" is a Japanese manga and anime series about Monkey D. Luffy, a young man who gains rubber-like powers after eating a Devil Fruit and sets out to become King of the Pirates by finding the legendary treasure known as the "One Piece". The series follows Luffy and his Straw Hat Pirates crew as they explore the Grand Line, encountering various characters, battling enemies, and facing challenges, all while seeking the ultimate treasure and striving to achieve their individual dreams.
The Roger Pirates, captained by Gol D. Roger, included several key members. Silvers Rayleigh served as the first mate, often called the "Dark King" and "Right Hand of the Pirate King". Scopper Gaban was another prominent member, known as the "Left Hand of the Pirate King" and the navigator. Crocus was their doctor, and Kozuki Oden joined them for a period of time. Shanks and Buggy were also part of the crew as cabin boys.
